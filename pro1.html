About Me

Hello! I'm Tarsha, a part-time photographer based in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. I picked up my first camera at the age of 14, and I've been captivated by the art of photography ever since.

I specialize in landscape and nature photography. There's something incredibly serene about capturing the beauty of the natural world. Whether it's the vibrant colors of a sunset or the intricate details of a flower, I aim to capture photos that make you feel like you're right there in the moment.

My philosophy when it comes to photography is simple: "Capture the extraordinary in the ordinary". I believe that there's beauty everywhere, you just have to look for it.

I currently shoot with a mobile phone ONEPLUS 9. While I believe that a great photo is more about the photographer than the gear, I do love the quality and versatility that my Phone provides provides.

If you'd like to book a shoot or if you have any questions, feel free to contact me. I can't wait to work with you!
